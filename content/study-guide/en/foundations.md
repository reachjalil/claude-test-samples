This portable content entry anchors the local study-guide corpus for the site. The
structured representation lives in `src/data/study-guide/foundations-guide.json`
so Astro pages, React islands, question metadata, and future question banks can
reuse the same blueprint without embedding exam content in component logic.

The current structure covers the guide's five weighted domains, six practice
scenarios, sample-question themes, task-statement knowledge and skills,
preparation exercises, appendix concepts, in-scope and out-of-scope boundaries,
and preparation recommendations.
